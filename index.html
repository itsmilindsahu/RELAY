How it works

01

Devices scatter and link up

Nodes are placed at random and connected to whichever others sit within radio range — just like phones discovering each other over Bluetooth or Wi-Fi Direct.

02

Every path is weighed

Rather than taking the shortest route, the simulator compares every reasonable path and scores each one by the average reliability of the devices along it.

03

The message hops through

The most trustworthy path wins. The message travels hop by hop, slowing down slightly at weaker devices, until it reaches its destination.
